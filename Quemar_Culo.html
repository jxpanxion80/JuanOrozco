Ingredients

  1. 6 medium jalapenos
  2. 1 quart vegetable broth
  3. 14oz plain egg noodles
  4. 3 tablespoons of cilantro
  5. 1 egg
  6. 2 limes halved and quartered

This dish can be served as a side or main meal.
Cut the jalapenos in 1/8th inch and place them in a sauce pan.
Heat the sauce pan and roast the jalapenos until they begin to brown.
Add vegetable broth and bring to a roaring boil.
Add noodles and egg to the boiling broth
Cook noodles to desired softness (6-8 minutes)
Add salt to taste
Place soup in bowl and add lime juice as desired
Enjoy!
