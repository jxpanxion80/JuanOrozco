Ingredients

  1. 1 can of Peaches in syrup
  2. 1 cup of diced red apples
  3. 1 cup of halved white grapes
  4. 1 cup of sliced bananas
  5. 1/4 cup of small marshmallows

Mix all ingredients in a large mixing bowl.
Pairs well with vanilla ice cream or whipped cream.
