Ingredients

  1. 1/2 cup of melted butter
  2. 1 tablespoon of salt
  3. 1 teaspoon of pepper
  4. 3 cloves of garlic
  5. 1/4 cup of olive oil
  6. 18 oz New York Strip or Shoulder cut

Take the melted butter and wait for it to cool and congeal.
In a separate bowl put all seasoning ingredients and mix
Take the steak and place it on a plate. Let it settle at room temp
Once steak is at room temp sprinkle the seasoning mix on the steak
*Begin to heat pan/skillet Once all the seasoning coats the steak add the steak in olive ole and garlic
Coat the steak with melted/soft butter and let sit until pan/skillet is heated
Place steak in heated pan/skillet slowly from handle to the edge
Cook to desired wellness(rare, medium rare, medium, medium well, well, well done) *For better result leave steak refrigerated in butter over-night and cook the next day
